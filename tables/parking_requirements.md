# Off-Street Parking Requirements

| Land Use | Parking Requirements |
|----------|---------------------|
| Dwelling, Single-Family | 2 spaces per dwelling unit |
| Dwelling, Two-Family | 2 spaces per dwelling unit |
| Dwelling, Multi-Unit | 1Â½ spaces per unit; plus 1 space per 4 units for visitors |
| Bed & Breakfast Inn | 1 space per guest room plus 2 spaces for the inn |
| Mobile Home Parks | 2 spaces per mobile home space |
| Boarding House | 1 space per 2 rooms for rent |
| Churches, Temples | 1 space per 3 seats in main auditorium |
| Libraries, Museums | 1 space per 300 sq. ft. of gross floor area |
| Hospitals | 1.5 spaces per bed |
| Nursing Homes | 0.35 spaces per bed |
| Medical & Dental Offices | 5 spaces per doctor plus 1 space per 200 sq. ft. |
| Banks | 1 space per 250 sq. ft. |
| General Offices | 1 space per 300 sq. ft. |
| Auto Service Stations | 1 space per 200 sq. ft. plus 4 stacking spaces per pump island |
| Retail Trade (General) | 1 space per 200 sq. ft. |
| Shopping Centers | 5.5 spaces per 1,000 sq. ft. |
| Restaurants & Taverns | 1 space per 3 seats |
| Drive-in Restaurants | 1 space per 100 sq. ft. plus 1 space per 3 seats |
| Automobile Sales | 1 space per 400 sq. ft. showroom + 1 per 1,000 sq. ft. lot area |
| Bowling Alleys | 5 spaces per lane |
| Theaters | 1 space per 3 seats |
| Elementary Schools | 1.5 spaces per classroom |
| High Schools | 1 space per 5 students designed capacity |
| Industrial | 1 space per 2 employees on largest shift |
