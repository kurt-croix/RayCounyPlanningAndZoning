# Article 90 - Perimeter Roadway Improvements

## 90.1 Authority and Purpose

This Article is enacted to ensure that new development contributes fairly to the cost of roadway improvements necessitated by the traffic impacts of such development on the county's transportation system.

## 90.2 Administration

The provisions of this Article shall be administered by the Planning and Zoning Administrator in coordination with the County Highway Administrator and other appropriate county officials.

## 90.3 Applicability

The requirements of this Article shall apply to:
1. All new subdivision developments
2. New commercial and industrial developments
3. Expansions of existing developments that generate additional traffic
4. Other developments as determined by the Planning and Zoning Administrator

## 90.4 Effect on Other Regulations

The requirements of this Article are in addition to, and not in lieu of, other applicable requirements of these Zoning Regulations and other county ordinances and regulations.

## 90.5 Improvements as Additional Requirement

Road impact fees and improvements required under this Article are in addition to other infrastructure requirements and shall not be credited against other development fees or improvement requirements unless specifically provided for in these regulations.

## 90.6 Construction of Improvements/Payment of Fees

Developers shall either:
1. Construct required roadway improvements in accordance with county specifications, or
2. Pay road impact fees as determined by the county, or
3. Enter into agreements for a combination of improvements and fees

## 90.7 Required Road Impact Fees and Road Improvements

### A. Fee Calculation
Road impact fees shall be calculated based on:
1. The type and intensity of proposed development
2. Expected traffic generation
3. Current condition of affected roadways
4. Cost of necessary improvements

### B. Improvement Requirements
Required improvements may include:
1. Roadway widening or reconstruction
2. Intersection improvements
3. Traffic control devices
4. Drainage improvements related to roadway construction

### C. Credit for Improvements
Developers who construct improvements exceeding the minimum requirements may receive credits against road impact fees for other developments, subject to county approval.

## 90.8 Deposition of Fees

Road impact fees collected under this Article shall be deposited in a dedicated fund for roadway improvements and shall be used only for transportation-related improvements in the area where the fees were collected.

## 90.9 Waiver of Required Improvements

The County Commission may waive or reduce required improvements or fees if:
1. The development provides other public benefits of equivalent value
2. Unusual circumstances make the requirements impractical or inappropriate
3. Alternative arrangements are made that adequately address traffic impacts
